To become a great jokesteur you need to pay attention to the following:

  1. Know your audience!
  2. Be confident! Even a bad joke comes out great if you tell it flamboyantly.
  3. Keep a log of your best jokes.
  4. Practice, practice, practice. If you don't have a human audience a cat or a house plant will do too!
